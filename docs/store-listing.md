## Full description (max 4000 chars)
App Opener and Timer opens the apps you choose, one at a time on a timer, and
tracks how long each one stays open. Build a checklist once, save it, and launch
the whole set with a single tap.

Great for routines where you open the same group of apps in sequence — morning
check-ins, testing, kiosk-style walkthroughs, streaming rotations, or any time
you'd rather not tap through the same apps by hand.

HOW IT WORKS
• Pick the apps you want from a checklist of everything installed.
• Save your selection as a named list — keep as many lists as you like.
• Tap Go, and the app opens your first pick, then the next one an interval later,
  and so on until the whole list has launched.
• Each app gets its own timer so you can see exactly how long it was open.

FEATURES
• Named app lists — create, open, edit, save, and delete as many as you want.
• Your lists are remembered between sessions.
• Set the seconds between launches per list (for example 30s for one list, two
  minutes for another).
• Live timers: running apps show a green, climbing timer; once you close an app
  its timer freezes and turns black so you can read the final time.
• A clear Running / Stopped indicator for the whole run.
• Start a one‑off selection without saving it, or run a saved list directly.

PRIVACY FIRST
• No accounts. No ads. No analytics. No third‑party tracking.
• Everything stays on your device — your lists never leave your phone.

PERMISSIONS
• "Display over other apps" lets the app launch the next app while it's running
  in the background.
• "Usage access" (optional) lets a timer stop the moment you close an app; without
  it, timers simply keep counting from launch.
• A foreground service and notification keep the launch schedule running.

Note: Android decides which app is on screen, not which apps are alive in the
background, so a timer stops when you leave an app and don't return — perfect for
the open‑it, use‑it, close‑it flow.
